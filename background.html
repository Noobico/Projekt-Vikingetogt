History & Background

The Gargan Clan, known for its fierce warriors and cunning strategists, emerged as a dominant force in the Viking era. With roots tracing back to the rugged northern fjords, Gargan warriors were skilled in both seafaring and combat. United by a code of loyalty and honor, the clan forged alliances, raided distant shores, and built a reputation for strength and resilience. Their settlements flourished as they adapted to both harsh winters and the challenges of the sea.
